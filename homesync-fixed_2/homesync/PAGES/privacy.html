Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026

HomeSync ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard data when you use the HomeSync platform.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account data: Name, email address, phone number, and password (stored encrypted).
  • Property data: Unit details, lease agreements, rent payment records, and maintenance tickets.
  • Usage data: Pages visited, features used, and time spent on the platform (anonymized).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the HomeSync service.
  • To send notifications about rent, tickets, and announcements.
  • To verify your identity and prevent unauthorized access.
  • To comply with legal obligations under Philippine law (RA 10173 — Data Privacy Act).

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with the landlord or tenant you are connected to within the platform, and with service providers necessary to operate HomeSync (e.g., email delivery). All third parties are bound by data processing agreements.

4. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, personal data is removed within 30 days, except where legally required to retain records.

5. Your Rights (RA 10173)

  • Right to access your personal data.
  • Right to correct inaccurate information.
  • Right to object to processing.
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten").

6. Security

We use industry-standard encryption and access controls. All passwords are hashed. We conduct regular security reviews.

7. Contact

For privacy concerns, email our Data Privacy Officer at privacy@homesync.ph or visit our Contact page.
