About HomeSync

HomeSync is a Philippine-based property management platform built to replace the chaos of SMS threads, manual spreadsheets, and paper receipts with a single, organized digital workspace.

🎯 Our Mission

To make property management simple, transparent, and accessible for every landlord and tenant in the Philippines — from single-unit landlords to large dormitory operators.

📖 Our Story

Founded in 2024, HomeSync started as a simple tool to help small landlords track rent payments. After talking to hundreds of landlords and tenants, we expanded into a full platform covering ticketing, leases, documents, and communication — all in one place.
