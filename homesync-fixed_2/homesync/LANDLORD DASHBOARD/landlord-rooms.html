🇵🇭 Philippine Special Discounts

Required by Philippine law and voluntary promos for special groups. Tenants must present valid ID.

👴
Senior Citizen Discount
RA 9994 — Expanded Senior Citizens Act. Required by law.
Discount Rate: % off monthly rent
Required ID: Senior Citizen ID (OSCA)
♿
PWD Discount
RA 10524 — Persons With Disabilities. Required by law.
Discount Rate: % off monthly rent
Required ID: PWD ID (PDAO)
🎓
Student Discount
Voluntary promo for enrolled students. Renewable each semester.
Discount Rate: % off monthly rent
Required: Valid school ID + Certificate of Enrollment (COE)
👩‍👧
Solo Parent Discount
RA 8972 — Solo Parents Welfare Act. Voluntary.
Discount Rate: % off monthly rent
Required: Solo Parent ID (DSWD)
🏛️
Government Employee
Promo rate for public sector workers. Voluntary.
Discount Rate: % off monthly rent
Required: Government Employee ID

⚖️ Legal Note on Philippine Discounts

  • Senior Citizen (20%) and PWD (20%) discounts are mandated by law (RA 9994, RA 10524). These cannot be disabled.
  • Only one special discount applies at a time (whichever is higher). Discounts cannot be stacked with each other.
  • Lease length discounts are separate and can be combined with one special discount.
  • Always verify IDs before applying discounts. Keep photocopies on file.
